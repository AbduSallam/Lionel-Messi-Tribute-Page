About Messi
Lionel Messi is an Argentian professional footballer that plays as a forward for FC Barcelona.

Born on June 24, 1987, in Rosario, Argentina, Lionel Messi moved from Argentina to Spain at the age of 13 to receive medical treatment after being diagnosed with a growth defect. Messi joined FC Barcelona, the club that offered to pay for his medical treatment, where he became one of the greatest footballers, helping the club win many trophies.
